# Banxico Market Note

## Objective
Develop a one-page market note assessing the implications of Banxico’s latest policy stance for MXN rates, inflation expectations, and USD/MXN.

## Project Overview
This project is a short market note focused on Banxico’s decision to keep the policy rate at 7.00% and its implications for the short end of the curve, the inflation outlook, and the durability of MXN carry.

The note is written in the style of a concise research or strategy update, combining monetary policy interpretation with market implications for rates and FX.

## Files
- `banxico_market_note.pdf` — final one-page market note

## Main Themes
- Banxico policy stance
- Inflation persistence and convergence path
- Front-end rates sensitivity
- MXN carry and FX implications
- Key risks for the next policy decision

## Key Takeaway
Banxico’s 7.00% hold is best interpreted as a restrictive pause rather than renewed tightening. For markets, the main issue is how inflation and communication affect the expected policy path, the short end of the curve, and support for MXN carry.
